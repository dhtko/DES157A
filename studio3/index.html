Instruction on Numeric Baseball

Numeric Baseball aims to find the four-digit hidden number in the strike zone within the tem attempts.

When you enter your four-digit guessing number and hit the "GO!" button, the guessing result will display on "Record." The result has three categories: ball(correct number, but wrong position), strike (correct number and correct position), and out(wrong number). Based on how many balls, strikes, and outs you got from the attempt, guess the number and enter another guessing number. If your guessing number matches to the hidden number, you won the game!

That is all the information need to enjoy the game. To refer to the instructions again, click the question mark in the top right corner.
